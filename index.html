Doctor Outreach Plan for QRS Treatment Cards

1. Find the Right Doctors

Focus on doctors who treat patients with pain, like back pain or joint pain. Start with general practitioners, orthopedic specialists, and wellness clinics.

2. Create a Small Info Pack

  • QRS Brochure: Explain that QRS is a safe, non-invasive German technology for pain relief.
  • Success Stories: Include stories of people who felt better using QRS.
  • Offer a Free Session: Let doctors try QRS to understand how it works.

3. Set Up Short Meetings

Call or visit doctors to schedule a quick 15-minute chat. Explain that QRS could help many of their patients feel better.

4. Show and Tell

Describe QRS simply: it’s a non-invasive therapy that helps reduce pain without surgery or medicine. Patients like it because it’s natural and painless.

5. Show How it Helps the Doctor

  • Extra Earnings: Offer a referral incentive for every patient sent to QRS.
  • Happy Patients: Satisfied patients build loyalty with the doctor’s clinic.
  • Exclusive Status: Early doctors get recognized as QRS partners.

6. Support the Doctors with Extra Materials

Provide handouts for patients, keep in touch regularly, and help answer any questions.

7. Track and Adjust

Check weekly progress, gather feedback, and make any necessary adjustments to improve doctor engagement and patient referrals.
